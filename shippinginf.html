Warranty & Protection

Your products are covered by Apple’s limited warranty and optional AppleCare+ plans.

Coverage details

1‑Year Limited Warranty included

Every Apple product comes with a one‑year limited warranty against manufacturing defects. It covers:

  • Hardware repairs and replacement parts
  • Service for battery defects (not normal wear)
  • Software support for the operating system
Complimentary 90‑day technical support included with every device.

AppleCare+ optional

Extend your coverage to 2 years and get additional protection:

  • Accidental damage coverage (₹1,500 – ₹2,500 service fee)
  • Battery replacement if capacity drops below 80%
  • 24/7 priority tech support via chat or phone

AppleCare+ can be purchased within 60 days of product activation.

How to claim

  1. Contact support via chat, email, or phone (see right panel).
  2. Describe the issue – our team will diagnose and advise.
  3. If repair is needed, ship the device (free pickup) or visit a service center.
  4. Repair or replacement is completed within 5–7 business days.
Pro tip: Have your serial number ready (Settings → General → About).
